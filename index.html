Welcome to the audio web portfolio of Evan W. O. Roberts. This is a showcase of my skills and talents as a voice actor, audio technician, sound designer, and web developer. I graduated from Spring Arbor University with a B. Sc. in Digital Media and Broadcasting and have worked at a number of different radio stations including 104.9 WCLV in Cleveland and 106.9 Home.FM in Michigan. I have created voice overs for professional training videos and trade videos and have voiced numerous characters for various media. My passion is telling stories and I would love to be the voice of your story.
